Before Fight

Surprised?

  • No one stealthy = No surprise
  • Stealth checks vs. passive perception
  • Surprised = can't take action or reaction until their first turn ends.

Establish positions

  • may use previous marching order
  • or ask players for preferred positions

Roll Initiative

  • groups may go as one
  • when players tie, they decide who goes first
  • player and monster tie = DM decides or rolls

Conditions

Blinded
fails ability checks requiring sight. Disadvantage on attacks. Attacks against with advantage.
Charmed
Can't harm charmer. Charmer has advantage on social interaction.
Deafened
fails ability checks requiring hearing.
Exhaustion
Not eating or drinking, 24 hours without long rest, traveling +8 hours, swimming +1 hour, some spells or creature abilities cause 1 or more level of exhaustion. 1 level of exhaustion is removed on long rest with food and water. You suffer current and all previous level.
1 Disadvantage on ability checks
2 Speed halved
3 Disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws
4 Hit point maximum halved
5 Speed reduced to 0
6 Death
Frightened
Disadvantage on checks and attacks while source of fear in sight. Can't willingly move closer to it.
Grappled
Speed 0. Ends if grappler incapacitated or removed from reach e.g. through Escape Action.
Incapacitated
Can't take actions or reactions.
Invisible
can't be seen without magic or special senses. Advantage on attack. Attacks against have disadvantage
Paralyzed
Incapacitated (no actions, reactions), can't move or speak. Attacks against have advantage. Any attack that hits is critical if attacker within 5ft. Fails STR and DEX saves.
Petrified
Incapacitated (no actions, reactions), can't move or speak, unaware of surroundings. Attacks against have advantage. Fails STR and DEX saves. Resistance to all damage. Immune to new poison and disease, existing ones are suspended. Weight times ten, cease aging.
Poisoned
Disadvantage on attacks and ability checks
Prone
Only crawl, unless standing up (costs half movement). Disadvantage when attacking. Attacks against have advantage if attacker within 5ft, otherwise disadvantage.
Resntrained
Speed 0. Disadvantage on attacks. Attacks against with advantage. Disadvantage on DEX saves.
Stunned
Incapacitated (no actions, reactions), can't move, speaks falteringly. Attacks against with advantage. Fails STR and DEX saves.
Unconscious
Incapacitated (no actions, reactions), can't move or speak, unaware of surroundings. Drops what they are holding, fall prone. Attacks against with advantage. Hit is critical if attacker within 5ft. Fails STR and DEX saves.

Concentration ends

  • if willfully dropped, when incapacitated or killed
  • another concentration spell is cast
  • when taking damage or environmental effects: DC 10 or half damage taken, whichever higher

Combat round

Movement

- split up and taken at any point in turn

Can't willingly end in another creature's space.

Another creature's space is difficult terrain (+5ft). Moving through a hostile creature only when two sizes larger or smaller.

Move 5ft for 5ft
Climb
Swim
Crawl		 10ft for 5ft
Drop prone 0ft
Stand up from prone Half movement
Difficult terrain +5ft per 5ft
Move with grappled speed halved

Action

1 per turn, more with Extra/Multi-Attack

Attack

roll melee or ranged attack: d20 + ability modifier (e.g. STR or DEX) + proficiency if greater or equal target AC = hits!

Damage: weapon damage + ability modifier

Extra/Multi-Attack

some features allow multiple attacks when attack action was taken.

When one-handed attack with light melee weapon, attacker can use bonus action for offhand attack (no ability modifier to damage unless a feat says otherwise)

Ranged Attacks

When shooting through ally's spaces, target has 1/2 cover unless using feats like sharpshooter or spell-sniper.

Advantage

on the attack when
  • against blinded, paralyzed, petrified, restrained, stunned, unconscious
  • melee against prone
  • attacker is invisible or hidden

Disadvantage

on the attack when
  • against invisible, hidden
  • ranged against prone
  • attacker is blinded, frightened, poisoned, restrained

Spells

- casting time 1 action

If a spell was or will be cast as bonus action, action spell must be a cantrip.

Some spells just take effect, others are require...

Spell Attack

d20 + spellcasting attack modifier (e.g. INT, WIS or CHAR modifier + proficiency) if greater or equal target AC = hits!

Saving Throw

target needs to make a saving throw against Spells Save DC 8 + spellcasting attack modifier (e.g. INT, WIS or CHAR modifier + proficiency)

Grapple

target no more than one size larger, within reach, one free hand. Athletics contested by targets Athletics or Acrobatics. Can replace one attack of multi-attack. On success: target grappled

Shove

target no more than one size larger, within reach, one free hand. Athletics contested by targets Athletics or Acrobatics. Can replace one attack of multi-attack. On success: target is pushed 5ft away.

Dash

increases movement by speed ft

Disengage

Movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks.

Dodge

until start of next turn attacks against have disadvantage if attacker in sight. Lost on incapacitated or speed 0.

Escape

from a grapple with Athletics or Acrobatics checck against grappler's Athletics.

Help

target gains advantage on next apility check or attack on creature within 5ft of helper. Until end of next turn.

Use object

one interaction (drawing weapon, opening door) free. Second one or object requiring an action takes this action.

Use shield

1 action to (un)equip.

Hide

Can't hide from creature that sees you. Must have total cover, heavily obscured area, invisibility. To hide Stealth Check. It's contested by Passive Perception or when actively searching PER check

Ready

choose what trigger causes what action (or move instead) as a reaction. Choice to react or ignore trigger. Readied spells consume spell slot even if not used.

Bonus action

max. 1 per turn

Offhand Attack

only after using Attack action with one-handed light melee weapon, using another light melee weapon in other hand. No ability modifier added to damage unless modifier is negative. Can use thrown property.

Spell

with 1 bonus action casting time. When casting a spell with bonus action can't cast a different spell other than cantrips with action.

Reaction

max. 1 per round

Opportunity attack

Trigger: enemy leaves reach. Can make one melee attack against provoking creature interrupting its movement. Not triggered on teleport or when no willing movement.

Cast a Reaction Spell

For specific spells and feats on specified triggers only.

when first attack choice to use, then advantage on melee weapon attacks using STR, but attack against have advantage until next turn.
